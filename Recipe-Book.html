Grilled Cheese!!!

  • Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 - 2 People
Ingredients
2 Slices of White Bread
2 Slices of Cheese
Salted Butter

Step 1: Heat one tablespoon of salted butter in a pan on medium-low heat.
Step 2: Put in your 2 slices of white bread, (make sure to have your cheese slices inside the bread), and press them down onto the pan. Do this for 3 - 5 minutes or until bread is golden.
Step 3: Flip over sandwhich and press for 3 - 5 minuts or until golden.

Pancakes!!!

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 3 People
Ingredients
2 Cups of Milk
1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
3/4 Cups of White Sugar
2 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
2 Eggs
1 1/2 Tablespoons of Baking Powder
1 Teaspoon of Vegetable Oil

Step 1: Add milk, sugar, oil, eggs and vanilla in a blender. Blend until smooth.
Step 2: Add flour and baking soda. Blend until smooth again.
Step 3: Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop 1/4 cup of the batter for each pancake.

Source: Pancake Recipe Website

Cookies!!!

  • Time: 25 Minutes
  • Serves: 5 People
Ingredients
2 3/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder
1 Cup Butter Softened
1 1/2 Cups of White Sugar
1 Egg
1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). In a small bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
Step 2: In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar and till properly mixed. Mix in egg and vanilla with beater. Slowly mix the ingrediants from the first bowl. Then use a teaspoon to put cookie-sized onto a cookie sheet.
Step 3: Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden.

Source: Cookie Recipe Website
