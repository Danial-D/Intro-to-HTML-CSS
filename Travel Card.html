Travel to Hawaii!!!

We all have wanted a vacation. Whether it was because we were just sick and tired of having to go to work everyday, or maybe because we just felt bored looking at the same scenery all the time. Regardless of why you've wanted a vacation, I know the perfect place to go. Hawaii!!!

Why is Hawaii So Great?

  • Total relaxtion. You get to stay at some of the world's best resorts and spas.
  • The amazing weather will definitely make you want to stay outside for the entire day, something you can't say up north.
  • The spectacular views will be forever imprinted into you're mind, you'll never forget them.
  • The fantastic food from many different countries, such as Vietnam and India, will make your mouth water.

What should I bring to Hawaii?

  • Sunglasses and sunscreen are essential for most vacations in tropical areas, and Hawaii is no exception.
  • With all the travelling you're gonna do, comfortable shoes are needed to make sure you can stay out and enjoy the great weather.
  • It's a great idea to take toiletries just in case your hotel doesn't provide them for free.
  • Take any prescription/non-prescription medicine to make sure you can stay healthy while on vacation.

This is my unordered list of things you should take to Hawaii. Remember though, you should only pack what you need. Packing too much can become inconvienient and annoying to carry around. Just pack the things I listed, plus some clothes, and you'll be fully prepared for your trip to Hawaii.
