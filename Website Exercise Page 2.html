What is Electronic Communication?

Electronic communication means any transfer of signs, signals, writing, images, sounds, data or intelligence of any nature transmitted by the use of electronic technology.

How to Electronically Communicate With Others?

You can electronically communicate with others through the use of e-mail, telephone, instant messaging, and other online services. There are many different ways to electronically communicate with others. However, keep in mind that different forms of electronic communication are appropriate for different situations. For example, if you want to communicate with a good friend, you should send a message through instant messaging. If you are going to communicate with an employer, it's better to use e-mail.
