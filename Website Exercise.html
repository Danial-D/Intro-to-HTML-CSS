What is Business Social Networking and Business Etiquette ?

Business social networking is the process of expanding and connecting one's business through social networks. An example of a social network is Twitter or Facebook. One can connect their business with e-mail, voice mail, instant messaging, and such. Business etiquette is the set of social guidlnes required when communicating with others in a business enviornment.

The Dangers of Social Media to Employees and Businesses

Social networks can help employees and businesses, but they can also severly damage them. This can happen when proper business etiquette is not used, which results in a tarnished reputation for the respective buisness/employee. This is why you should always be careful when on social media, otherwise you may not have a job after that latest facebook post. An example of improper buisness etiquette is Donald Trump.
